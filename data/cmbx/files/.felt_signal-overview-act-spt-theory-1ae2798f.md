Hero figure for the Euclid × CMB-κ cross-correlation analysis. Six panels per method (one per tomographic bin) showing ℓ·C_ℓ for ACT DR6 and SPT-3G GMV, with Planck 2018 theory overlay. Three methods (lensmc, metacal, density) produce three figures + three redshift trend panels.

**Density × κ.** Strong detection in every bin. ACT excess S/N: 3.1, 6.3, 6.8, 6.7, 11.9, 9.7σ. SPT: 5.1, 10.7, 10.4, 4.3, 7.9, 15.9σ. Theory tracks data well (A_lens ≈ 1.05 ACT, 0.86 SPT).

**Shear × κ.** After the pol_conv sign convention fix, shear is also strongly detected. LensMC ACT per-bin: 1.5, 0.7, 3.1, 7.1, 12.2, 17.7σ — signal rises sharply with redshift as expected from lensing kernel overlap. A_lens ≈ 0.88 (ACT), 0.69 (SPT). SPT deficit is consistent across all tracers (~15-20% low).

**ACT-SPT consistency.** Both experiments see the same spectral shape across all bins. Detailed chi2 consistency in [ACT vs SPT](.felt/act-vs-spt-cross-spectra-b4181199.md).
