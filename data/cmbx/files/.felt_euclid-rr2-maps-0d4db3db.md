Euclid RR2 weak lensing and galaxy clustering maps used in the cross-correlation analysis. Two shear measurement pipelines (lensmc, metacal), galaxy density from the GC catalog, and 11 VMPZ-ID systematic maps. The footprint covers ~385 deg² across two spatial patches. Masking follows spectra.make_mask: weights multiplied into the binary footprint, then C2 apodized.

## Shear maps

- **Pipelines**: lensmc, metacal — independent shape measurement methods
- **Per-bin products**: e₁/e₂ shear maps, sum_weights, n_eff
- **All-bin**: union across `config[tomography.bin_ids]`
- **Sign convention**: Euclid e₁ along RA; HEALPix Q along colatitude → [-e₁, e₂] for NaMaster (pol_conv=COSMO)
- **Noise**: NOISE_CL in FITS headers = σ²_comp / n_eff_sr (flat shape noise per component)

## Galaxy density maps

- **Source catalog**: `config[gc_catalog]` (parquet)
- **Bins**: `config[gc_tom_bins]`
- **Products**: δ overdensity + selection mask per bin
- **Density mask is strictly binary**: `build_density_maps.py` sets mask = 1 for every occupied pixel, 0 elsewhere. Only one distinct non-zero value (confirmed bin=3). `make_convergence_mask` then applies `apodize(mask > 0) * mask`, which is apodize(binary) * 1 — the weight factor contributes nothing extra (contrast with shear where `weights=[weight_map]` tapers by varying galaxy weights).
- **Shot noise**: N_ℓ = 1/n̄_sr (constant per mode)

## CMB lensing mask apodization

`make_cmb_mask` calls `nmt.mask_apodization(mask, aposcale, "C2")` on the input. The ACT DR6 baseline mask is already the official 3° cos² apodized lensing mask (Qu+ 2023 Sec 5.3). Applying a second 0.15° C2 pass changes mask pixel values by at most 6.3×10⁻⁴ (rms 2.6×10⁻⁵), zero change in fsky — negligible. This is benign: the long-scale ACT apodization dwarfs the 0.15° second pass.

## Systematic maps

11 VMPZ-ID maps at nside 16384 (NESTED), downgraded to analysis nside at load time:
stellar_density, galactic_extinction, exposures, zodiacal_light, saa, psf, noise, persistence, star_brightness_mean, galaxy_number_density, phz_quality

## Tomographic binning

`config[tomography.n_bins]` bins (IDs: `config[tomography.bin_ids]`). Shear n(z) from `config[nz.wl]`; density n(z) from `config[nz.gc]`.

## Weight masking

spectra.make_mask(): binary footprint × weights, then apodize. Scale: `config[cross_correlation.aposcale]`°. For density, weights=[binary mask], so C2 apodization acts on a hard edge — visible rolloff at the footprint boundary. For shear, weights=[sum_weights], so the taper is smoothed by varying galaxy counts before apodization.

## Estimator

`config[cross_correlation.estimator]`: catalog (NmtFieldCatalog, bypasses map-making) or map (NmtField).
