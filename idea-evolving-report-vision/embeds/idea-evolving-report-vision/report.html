The evolving
report.

The agent's cognition is not a transcript. It is a manipulable artifact — renderable, addressable, typed — that the worker maintains and the human steers.

Brainstormed in an interactive Shuttle dispatch on constitution-tapestries-publish, with prompts from Thariq, François, Kangning, and the Symphony team. First consequence already landed: the constitution itself was restructured into the new shape.

§ I · Thesis

Chat is no longer the medium.

We have been treating chat as the gravitational center of working with Claude — files, fibers, code, the kanban orbit the conversation and the conversation carries them by reference. Tonight, four threads converged on the opposite move: the conversation orbits the work, not vice versa.

What replaces chat-as-medium is an artifact the worker maintains and the human reads, annotates, and steers. The artifact lives somewhere durable — a fiber's body, a rich HTML companion, a typed reasoning graph — and chat shrinks to the back-and-forth anchored to specific parts of it. Plural, contextual, placed.

For the artifact to bear that weight, it needs three properties. Renderable: the human can see it richly (vellum's job). Addressable: the human can talk about specific parts (anchored chats, annotations). Typed: operations on it are constrained and meaningful — not "I don't like this," but "exclude option X with reason Y." Without typing, the back-channel collapses to free-text prose the next worker has to interpret. With it, iteration closes structurally.

§ II · Architecture

The loop, drawn in one breath.

Task at the top, loop closes through the artifact at the bottom. The orchestrator dispatches a worker; the worker produces a typed graph of cognition; the renderer composes it from primitives into an interactive steerable report; the human's typed amendments feed back to the next dispatch.

§ IV · Primitives

Four pieces of substrate.

Stripping the architecture to its parts. Each primitive has a clear job and a clear relationship to the others.

substrate

Fibers

Concerns. The unit of named thing. Directory-contained markdown with YAML frontmatter; relationships through containment, wikilinks, and project-owned conventions. Felt owns this layer.

work product

Artifacts

Rich rendered outputs — typed reasoning graphs plus free-form prose, plus plots, HTML companions, recordings. Lives in fiber dirs or in the body inline. Vellum + Mystra render.

conversation

Chats

Anchored, plural, contextual. Attach to a fiber, an artifact span, a running dispatch, a kanban card. Not the front door — the back-and-forth around specific things.

orchestration

Dispatch

Shuttle. Watches the fiber tree, dispatches workers per eligible fiber, handles lifecycle (oneshot, standing, awaiting review, tempered). Consumes typed amendments from the artifact as input to the next dispatch.

§ V · Trajectory

How we get there from here.

Each step is independently useful. Each makes the next easier. Step 1 already landed tonight.

Constitution as evolving report

Restructure the constitution body into a status report with the spec inline as reference: Current State / Findings / Open Questions on top, Desired State / Context below. Plots and media embed via markdown image syntax. The body becomes the single document; the kanban outcome shrinks to a CLI headline. Shuttle skill updated; constitution-tapestries-publish migrated as the first instance.

Typed decisions in Findings

When a non-trivial decision lands during dispatch, the worker writes it as a structured YAML entry inside ## Findings — decision, options, chosen, rationale, evidence. Free-form notes stay for the small stuff. The same body holds both surfaces. No new infrastructure; just convention plus vellum's existing YAML-in-markdown rendering.

Multiverse as branchpoint

Extend ASTRA's multiverse so new decision points can be added by a worker during dispatch, not only declared up-front in astra.yaml. Each unspecified choice the worker hits becomes a branch; the worker implements the preferred branch and leaves the alternatives live for the human to promote later. Kangning's framing, made concrete in lightcone.

Typed annotations as back-channel

Vellum's annotation surface grows typed gestures for structured entries: exclude option, promote branch, anchor insight, challenge rationale. Free margin notes stay for the prose. The amendments serialize into the next dispatch's context as typed input, not free text. The loop closes structurally.

Mystra primitives, agent composes

The agent stops writing raw HTML and instead emits compositions of typed Mystra primitives — compare-universes, decision-tree, before-after-diff, interactive-slider. The renderer realizes them with affordances. Separation of cognition from affordance; the renderer gets better over time without retraining the model.

§ VI · Open questions

What's not yet settled.

Three threads stay open. Each waits for pressure from the work to force its answer; none is blocking the next move.

Where does the structured-reasoning surface live?

For now: free-form prose in the report's sections, plus markdown image embeds for plots and media. Typed entries (ASTRA-style YAML inside Findings, or as separate decision sub-fibers) wait for pressure from the work — once a fiber has enough load-bearing decisions that the free-form treatment loses signal, the typing will earn its place.

What's the minimum Mystra primitives set?

The framing is "expose Mystra primitives and let Claude compose the page." Step 5 of the trajectory. The set hasn't been specified — we don't yet know what shapes recur enough to justify factoring. The honest answer: write a few more rich reports, watch which shapes appear repeatedly, then promote them.

Does this absorb into Shuttle, or stay a parallel layer?

Shuttle is the dispatcher; the evolving report is the work product. They compose cleanly already — Shuttle dispatches a worker against a constitution, the worker maintains report.html, the human reads. The boundary needs testing in practice before we know whether report-handling deserves first-class treatment inside Shuttle or stays a vellum-level concern.
